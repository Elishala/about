About Me

I believe that life is short & that it is our responsibility to participate in making it beautiful. Good design is beautiful. Nature is beautiful. Authenticity is beautiful. At heart, I design. I design & I observe. I find that my detailed sensibilities work well with design & project management. I appreciate being challenged because I believe that's when the magic of being & creating something inspiring has potential. Some other things about me, I'm a dreamer. I love huskies. I'm working on being a runner. I teach yoga. Yoga helps ground me, iron me out, & is something I return to. I watch other people learn web development & I see how it clicks for them. It doesn't quite click like that for me. I studied Front-End Engineering at The Iron Yard. For 12 weeks I spent 70-80 hours a week studying HTML, CSS, UX/UI, & JavaScript. At work I use PHP in WordPress custom made themes. Although I'm not comfortable writing JS logic, I love being inside a text editor.
